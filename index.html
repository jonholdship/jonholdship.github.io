HappyTrack

HappyTrack is a simple Android app I wrote to try out material design guidelines and as a way to practice organizing my code. If you like collecting data on yourself, HappyTrack will give you daily reminders to rate your day on a scale of 1-10 and to optionally keep notes. The resultant database is then available to the user along with some simple analysis done by the app.

Character Sheet

A D&D 3.5e character sheet in handy Android app format. The app automatically calculates bonuses and fills out class features so you only have to fill in the most basic aspects of your character. After that, watch your entire sheet update for any minor change. DM turns you in a troglodyte? Alter your Dex and Con and watch your skills drop and your hitpoints rise. This example is for illustration and not at all the experience that produced this app.

PyD&D

Python/GTK program for DMs. Design and run encounters without calculating anything. Simply fill out the basic details of your party and build encounters by filtering through the entire SRD monster list. Encounter levels, XP and treasure rewards are all calculated according the DM's guide tables and presented in the encounter builder so you know exactly what encounters and rewards are appropriate.
