What It Is

PyD&D is an free, open source python app for designing and running encounters. In three separate tabs you design encounters, manage your party, and run saved encounters.

PyD&D is available on GitHub and requires GTK 3+ and python to run.



Parties and Encounter Levels

The encounter design tab lets you search and filter the entire SRD monster list to find the perfect opponents for your party. You can search by CR, type, size or even location when looking for inspiration. A search bar allows you to quickly find monsters you want.

The encounter designer also combines monster CRs to give encounter levels and uses the ECL of your current party members to calculate the average XP per character and the treasure that should be awarded according to the 3.5e DMG. To do this, you simply fill out the basic details of your party in the party tab.



Run it!

In the final tab, you can run any encounter you've designed and saved. The battle tab shows your party and enemies in two separate lists for you to keep track of initiative and HP. It also displays the SRD stats of each enemy is displayed on screen so you know exactly what your monsters can do. Pressing start rolls initiative for anyone you haven't already filled in and then highlights the character who will go first.
