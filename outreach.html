Outreach


I've always been a huge fan of outreach both in terms of personal enjoyment and the positive effect it has. For the past two years, I have delivered a public lecture on star formation as part of the excellent Science Centre Lectures series.


I've also been taking part in the ORBYTS programme which places early career researchers and PhD students in secondary schools to lead GCSE and A-level students in original research. We've even successfully published a paper based on my first group's work!


I'm always open to outreach opportunities so please do email me!
