Prison Education


I'm currently spending 3 months at Meganexus , a company who deliver education to prisons throughout the UK. I have been working on recommendation problems, using neural networks to recommend educational courses and job opportunities to prisoners based on their education history.


NHS - Frailty


An ageing population is a huge problem for the NHS and we need to adapt the way we provide care to cope with it. One issue is frailty: the decline of a person's overall wellbeing so that minor setbacks lead to hospitalization and long term illness. A data focused approach to identifying frail people will allow for early intervention as a patient starts to show risk factors. This could lead to an overall reduction in the proportion of the population that is considered severely frail.


Funded by the STFC, I will be on secondment with the NHS for six months of 2019. I will be working on a model to predict frailty from the data that is collected as standard for every patient who visits a hospital or GP. The aim will be to identify patients who would benefit from intervention before they become frail and to flag or classify patients who are likely to be frail.
