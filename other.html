Guys and St Thomas - NHS


I was funded by the STFC to work for GSTT for six months at the end of my first Postdoc. We worked on predictive models that with direct operational applications. For example, we recently developed a model that could rank all future outpatient appointments at the trust by the likelihood of the patient actually attending. This allows administrative staff at outpatient clinics to chase patients who are likely to miss their appointment.


I now work part time for GSTT as a data scientist, continuing the work we were doing and moving on to new projects. We hope to produce tools that will allow doctors to estimate how long their patients will be on the wards and to highlight patients who are a fall risk.


Prison Education


I've recently spent 3 months at Meganexus , a company who deliver education to prisons throughout the UK. I worked on recommendation algorithms for the content they provide. We used content keywords to group similar courses as well as neural networks for individual recommendations.

In particular, we used an autoencoder that was trained to take a list of ratings for all courses (including 0s for unrated courses) and then return the same list. However, we didn't penalize the network for it's guesses for empty ratings and as a result, it learned to fill them in with a best guess based on what it had learned from other users.
