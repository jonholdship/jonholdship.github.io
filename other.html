GSTT NHS Foundation Trust


I worked for Guy's and St Thomas NHS Foundation trust for the second half of 2019 through a Dirac funded fellowship. Following that, I have taken on a permanent, part time role at the trust as a data scientist working directly with clinicians to improve access to healthcare for all the trust's patients.


In work such as predicting who will miss their appointments so administrative staff can intervene, we find practical ways to make operational predictions that will greatly improve patient care.


Prison Education


During my time as a PDRA at UCL, I spent 3 months workign with one of their industry partners at Meganexus , a company who deliver education to prisons throughout the UK. I worked on recommendation problems, using neural networks to recommend educational courses and job opportunities to prisoners based on their education history.
